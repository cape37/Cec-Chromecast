1. Introduction

   For the purposes of this document, an fylleconf is the method of 
   configurating networking equipment & IT Systems but also programing
   under heavy influence of alcohol with the purpose of reching 
   ballmer peak working condition. 
   
   The ballmer peak was discoverd by microsoft in the late 80's. 
   The casue is unkown, But somehow a BAC. Between 0.129% and 
   0.138% confers superhuman programing and configration ability.

   This document describes fylleconf and how to stay at the ballmer
   peak for longer durations of time with minimal side effects and 
   how to optimize and manage a group of individuals at the 
   ballmer peak. But also how to the ultimate path to ballmers peak 
   and getting drifting indviduals back to to ballmers peak. 

2. Motivation

   The requirments for configuration & programing have reached beyond 
   anyones expectaion. The ammount of time and skill required to 
   search and copy from stackoverflow(SO) and stackexchange(SE) 
   have multiplied over a 100x times over just a few years and is 
   expected to keep rising. This puts absurd level of pressure 
   and stress on indivduals working in the field. 
   
   The only possibile solution to this problem is reducing the reliance 
   of SE & SO. This requiers original configuration and programing. 
   Completley without copying. This is simply inhuman and impossible 
   without external help.
   
   For the survival of IT as a whole we can't stress enough about the 
   importance of Implementing fylleocnf as the norm in IT. 
   
   1 hour fylleconf of usualy generates the same amount of OG code 
   that the same individual would make in 40 hours of configuration.

2. Planning

   A usual fylleconf lasts for 12 hours with 8-10 hours of effective 
   work at ballmers peak. The recomendation is to start around 18:00
   and ends at 6:00. This is the estimated norm but there will be some 
   calibration needed for eatch individual. 
   
   Getting experinced can take alot of time and trail and error. 
   Usally it takes an individual around two years of experince for the
   abillity to manage his alcohol intake for optimal time at ballmers 
   peak. When using inexperinced fylleconfare it is recomended to have 
   a manager checking the alcohol level using a dräger. To make sure they 
   and then use his experince to reduce or incresse the intake. 
   During a individuls first fylleconfs a dual pipe drinking hat can be
   used. Where the manager controls all intake by pouring the alcohol 
   into the hat. 
   
   Automation we have started to se a trend automating fylleconf using a 
   dualpipe drinking hat & fishtank pumps contolled by blockchain cloud AI.
   With an IoT alchol blood test attached to the individual. Many simple
   ones can be find on ebay for less then $10 000. Thesse can only mix 
   mountan dew and vodka. That is suffefienct for most individuals. 
   But more advanced models allows for swapping the ingredients and 
   optimizing fore more individuals. But when looking at advanced models.
   Be careful since many require proprietary mixers.
